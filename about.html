Our story

Real Travelling is the latest venture by leading travel experts Gap 360 Ltd, a 100% independent youth travel specialist

Gap 360 Ltd was founded in 2011 by its three directors: James Jeremy, Jane Mclellan and David Stitt, who have more than 50 years collective experience in gap year travel and adventure trips.

Our philosophy is simple: we want to give you an excellent experience at a reasonable price, and help you find the unforgettable adventure, bucket list trip or and round-the-world tour you’ve been searching for.

We’ve researched hundreds of trips around the world to find the best experiences for our travellers. Our team is made up of travel experts and enthusiasts, with first-hand experience of solo and group travel, so you will always have the support you need.

Our organisation is consistently rated as one of the best in the industry by customers due to the level of service we provide. In addition, we are a member of the travel consumer protection association ABTOT, meaning your money is protected and your trip is financially secure.

Our flights team is ready to help you book the best flight for trip, and we'll also help you with travel insurance.
